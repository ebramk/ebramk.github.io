Welcome to Saint Mark's First Group Hymns Class Website

This website was built specifically for the 3-7 year old children of the Coptic Orthodox Church of Saint Mark in Jersey City, NJ. Lord willing, this website will assist the parent in reviewing the various hymns and songs being taught to the children in our weekly meeting. The website will also remain up to date with all the latest class news and updates. To get started, simply click on the grade where your son or daughter belongs to view all the hymns being taught in class. 

Please pray for the service!
